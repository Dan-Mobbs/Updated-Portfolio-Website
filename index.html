Bay Trust Radio

The bay Trust radio charity needed a new website, and I'm offered my skills voluntarily as a web developer. I meet with Danny and John, the station manager, and we discussed the project and ended up on a plan. The charity needed a website application with full radio functionality, such as music updates, presenter on-air and a news feed. I decided a WordPress website with plugins would do the job and pick a pre-made WordPress template with the plugins already integrated within the theme. I worked with Bay trust radio for six months, building the website and assisting with other web-related projects.

"I've struggled to find the right people to help me with my website. Dan listens to what you want and delivers what you ask for (usually earlier than the milestone deadlines) with great quality."

RaceHorse Rescue

I meet with John, the owner of Carlisle racehorse centre. He needed his website updating and also a members-only area creating for the donating members of the charity. We discussed the project and how we would go about it, and then I started to plan out the project. The charity website being a WordPress website, I decided instead of coding the project from scratch, I would use plugins to build out the membership area. After one month, I completed the project and built a membership area that included ways for the members to get individual posts, images and news for the charity and make payments through the website.
